Story Time

Saw my parents struggling to pay all the bills so I left studies and stepped in. Royal Cakes was formed in 2024 in South Africa known for their delicious and gorgeous cakes, cupcakes and pastries.

Royal Cakes makes products for weddings, birthdays and every special occasion having creative designs all made with love. The Royal comes with high quality ingredients and detailed attention and presentation. Promising to serve every customer with joy and care.

Mission

To make unique tasteful products to the customers to bring joy and love to every event. We promise to put the best effort in baking, only using high quality ingredients and putting care in our products and creating new designs that customers love .

Vision

To be known as a leading bakery brand in South Africa. Also having an ecofriendly face by using 100% recyclable packages and save water and energy during production.

Always improving in our processes and providing high quality, delicious baked products to put happiness in every customer.

Team members

Our team are fully skilled bakers, decorators and staff working as a unit to created tasty products and excellent customer service.

Each member is passionate, committed and dedicated to make every cake, cupcake and pastry having high standards of quality.

Our Values

  • Quality: Quality always comes first at Royal Cakes. Best ingredients are always picked. Satisfying our customer fully by baking with care and following high standards while making cakes, cupcakes and pastries.
  • Creativity: All our products have meanings. Having skilled team members with talents and passion creating tasty and fresh products. This makes every bite at events memorable and enjoyable.
  • Care: Every step of baking is made with love. From mixing to decorating. Royal cakes have kind and friendly service. To make every customer feel special we pay close attention to their requirements.
